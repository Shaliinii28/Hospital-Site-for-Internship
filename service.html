Our Departments


  • OUTPATIENT DEPARTMENT :  The OP facility in the hospital may have tests, treatments or surgery and the patients can stay a few hours or even overnight.
  • PAEDIATRIC :  Medical speciality that manages medical conditions affecting babies,children and young people.
  • CARDIOLOGY :  The field includes medical diagnosis and treatment of congenital heart defects, heart failure etc.
  • DENTAL :  Medical speciality that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of conditions of oral cavity
  • PHYSIOTHERAPY :  Treatment of injury or deformity by physical methods such as massage, exercise etc.
  • OPHTHALMOLOGY :  Medical speciality for diagnosis, treatment and prevention of diseases of eye and visual system.
  • ENT :  Specialty within medicine that deals with medical conditions of the ear, nose, and throat.
  • PSYCHIATRY :  Psychiatry focuses on the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of mental disorders.
