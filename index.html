Welcome to SBS Hospital


We Have Medicare Plan Options for You!

The basic objective of the hospital is to provide modern health services to the poor and backward class of the society, at an affordable cost. It also aimed at supplementing the efforts of the Public Authorities in providing health services to the society at large without the exploitation as is being experienced from private hospitals. We provide Modern Health Services to the poor and backward class of the society, at an affordable cost and also provide such services to the marginalized sections such as mother and children, migrant labour, geriatic patients etc. at concessional rates.

Our Facilities

  • Casualty: The Casualty Department provides a 24-hour emergency service, 365 days a year, and sees in the region of 50,000 patients annually.
  • Pharmacy: The Pharmacy provides a 24x7 service in our Inpatient Block .We provide expert knowledge in managing your drug therapy.
  • Laboratory: The Laboratory of our hospital is a comprehensive provider of laboratory testing and expert medical interpretation of specimens.
  • Ambulance: Hospital provides 24-hours Emergency Ambulance services for urgent medical care and transport services.
  • Theatre: Our operation theatres are well equipped for gynecology and orthopaedic departments to carry out surgical operations.
